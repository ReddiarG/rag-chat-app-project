      GarageBand

      The easy way to make incredible music.

      Make music

      GarageBand turns your Mac, iPad, and iPhone into a full-featured recording studio — so you can make music anywhere you go. Use Multi‑Touch to play keyboards and guitars, create beats, and more. And with Live Loops, have fun creating music like a DJ.

      Edit and mix

      Tons of helpful recording and editing features make GarageBand for Mac as powerful as it is easy to use. Edit your performances right down to the note and decibel. Fix rhythm issues with a click. Finesse your sound with audio effect plug‑ins. And finish your track like a pro, with effects such as compression and visual EQ.

      Start a song anywhere

      Wherever you are, iCloud makes it easy to work on a GarageBand song. You can add tracks to your GarageBand for Mac song using your iPhone or iPad when you’re on the road. Or when inspiration strikes, you can start sketching a new song idea on your iOS device, then import it to your Mac to take it even further.
