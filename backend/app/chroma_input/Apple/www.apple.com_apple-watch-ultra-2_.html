    Apple Watch Ultra 2

    New finish. Never quit.

    Featuring a stunning black titanium case, Apple Watch Ultra 2 is the ultimate sports and adventure watch. It has all the connectivity, health, and safety features for the everyday. And it takes training further with the most accurate GPS in a sports watch.1

    Watch the film

    Design

    Survival of the toughest.

    Ultra 2 is built for extremes. Available in a black or natural titanium finish, the 49mm case is designed for the toughest environments, and it’s certified to EN13319, the internationally recognized standard for diving accessories.

    View in AR

    Action on the spot. The customizable Action button gives you precise control over starting a workout, marking a segment, or moving on to the next interval.

    Battery life for miles. Wherever you find yourself, the last thing you want to worry about is your battery. Ultra 2 is engineered to perform at peak conditions longer, so you can perform at yours.4

    Running

    Personal bests made better.

    Apple Watch Ultra 2 helps you train smarter, to get results faster. Precision dual-frequency GPS utilizes advanced satellite and signal models, custom positioning algorithms, and Apple Maps data to provide the most accurate GPS in a sports watch.1 Along with advanced metrics — the pavement doesn’t stand a chance against you.

    Discover more about your perfect running partner.

    • Go all out on insights. Training load provides helpful insights to make it easier for you to see the impact your workouts have on your body over time. So it’s easier to plan when to push and when to recover — especially important when you’re training for a big event.

    • You’re so in the zone. Heart Rate Zones quickly give you a sense of your intensity level. Training zones are automatically calculated and personalized using your health data, or you can create them manually.

    • Detects when you’re right on track. When you select an outdoor run, Ultra 2 automatically detects if you’re at a track — an industry first — and provides incredible accuracy for your pace, route, map, and distance.

    • Pace with precision. Stay on track with features like Pacer and Race Route. Pacer lets you pick a distance and time target for an outdoor run. With Race Route you can race against your last or best time on your favorite runs.

    • Exercise control. Create a Custom Workout to include a warmup, repeating work and recovery intervals, and a time to cool down. And get in-the-moment guidance to take you through the workout.

    Cycling

    Find a whole new gear.

    Apple Watch Ultra 2 is the perfect partner whether you’re riding indoors, road cycling, mountain biking, or commuting. From Bluetooth‑enabled accessories to metrics to seeing them easily while you’re riding, you’re always ready to roll.

    Great ways to pick up the speed.

    • MPH, ASAP. Workout views help you keep an eye on your metrics. You can see your current speed right where you need it. Turn the Digital Crown for more metrics like Heart Rate Zones, splits, and elevation.

    • Your metrics — on the big screen. Start a cycling workout from Ultra 2 and it automatically shows up as a Live Activity on your iPhone — making it easier to see and safer when it’s mounted on your handlebars.

    • Sensor pairing. Pair Ultra 2 to Bluetooth-enabled accessories to give you key metrics like cadence and power. It’s as easy as riding a bike.

    • Power Zones. When a connected power meter is detected, Ultra 2 automatically estimates your Functional Threshold Power (FTP), the highest level of intensity you can theoretically maintain for an hour. Your FTP enables Power Zones, which show what zone you’re in and track how long you spend in each one to improve your performance.

    Swimming

    A stroke of genius.

    You’ve got total control in the pool or ocean with Apple Watch Ultra 2. Create your own Custom Workout and track your intervals. Get all the advanced features you need and more. Welcome to life in the fast lane.

    Features that make waves.

    • Pool or open water swim. Whether you’re in the ocean or in a pool, Ultra 2 will automatically detect your stroke, calculate auto sets with distance and pace, and provide advanced metrics like SWOLF. And you can see the water temperature right from your workout.

    • Multisport. It’s three of a kind. Ultra 2 automatically transitions between swimming, cycling, and running, and records your metrics for each.

    • Freestyle your workouts. Custom Workouts let you tailor workouts to your needs. You are automatically guided through timed intervals of work and recovery in the pool — with haptics to let you know when it’s time to move to the next interval.

    • See what’s Up Next. Know exactly what’s coming with the Up Next view. For any Custom Workout you create, Ultra 2 allows you to see what’s remaining in your current interval and what you’ll be tackling next.

    • Custom Workouts from third-party apps. Seamlessly sync Custom Workouts from third-party apps to your Ultra 2 to see your personalized workouts directly in the Workout app.

    Hiking

    Push past your peak.

    When you’re limited by what you can bring, the right tools are essential. With Apple Watch Ultra 2 at your side, you have everything you need for every situation you face. With a powerful compass, offline maps, and route creation, now you’ve got the backcountry on the back of your wrist.

    Packed with essentials.

    • A compass that pushes the needle. To get back on route, Backtrack uses GPS data to automatically create a path of where you’ve been, even if you’re off the grid. Easily add waypoints for anything of interest, like the trailhead or your campsite.

    • Make your own trails. Create and save custom walking and hiking routes with just a few taps. Choose different route options, including one way, out and back, or a loop. You’ll also get turn-by-turn directions, which means you have everything you need to navigate right from your wrist.

    • Take a hike. The Hiking workout lets you track pace, distance, elevation gain, and calories burned. You can see how high you’ve climbed in real time during the workout, and total elevation gain at the end of your workout.

    • Leave your iPhone behind. Take directions with you. Download offline maps to your iPhone, and they will automatically sync to your watch.7 So you’re free to let Ultra 2 lead you every step of the way.

    • X marks the spot to make a call. Cellular waypoints are automatically added to the Compass app, so you can retrace your steps to make a call or send a text, or even call emergency services.8

    Diving

    You’re never out of your depth.

    Whether it’s scuba diving, free diving, snorkeling, or high-speed water sports, Apple Watch Ultra 2 is made for it all. Certified to EN13319 and capable of diving to 40 meters, it’s got all the core features you need to break new ground in the water.

    Dive into more details.

    • A full dive computer at hand. Designed in partnership with Huish Outdoors, the Oceanic+ app turns Ultra 2 into a fully capable dive computer running a Bühlmann decompression algorithm.9 Simply turn the Digital Crown to access additional screens that make complex information easy to understand. And you’ll get all the safety warnings you’d expect, while the app constantly calculates and monitors your dive parameters.

      Get the Oceanic+ app

    • Location is everything. Plan your dive using Location Planning and the Oceanic+ app on iPhone. Heat maps help you find popular spots, while providing local conditions with community feedback about the area.

    • Come up for air and info. Ultra 2 syncs automatically to the Oceanic+ app on your iPhone and provides data from your dive quickly after surfacing, including a summary of your dive profile, and critical dive parameters. It also makes it easy to search through past dives and pictures and share your experiences with friends and family.

    • Go with the flow. The Tides app shows seven days of information including rising, falling, high, and low tides, sunrise, and sunset. And you can check conditions at shorelines around the world, or search over 115,000 beaches by name.

    • Dark. Art. The Titanium Milanese Loop is inspired by stainless steel mesh historically used by divers. Made from woven, corrosion-resistant titanium wire, it’s flattened and polished to a beautiful satin texture. The parachute-style buckle has dual side-release buttons that close with an audible click. While rugged enough for scuba, it’s also elegant enough to wear while out at night.

    More sports

    It’s great in every arena.

    Apple Watch Ultra 2 is the best sports watch for so many more activities, like skiing, snowboarding, rowing, and golf. Check out the App Store for tons of additional ways to take sports to the next level.

    Health

    Knows you more. Shows you more.

    The more insight you have, the more empowered you are. Advanced features like the Vitals app give you a better understanding of your daily health status. As always, your data is kept private and secure. Along with other innovations in Apple Watch Ultra 2, you’ve got more health insights, in sight, than ever.

    More ways to stay informed, head to toe.

    • Vital for insights. The Vitals app establishes a typical range for each of your overnight health metrics, like your heart rate, respiratory rate, wrist temperature, and sleep duration.10 A quick glance lets you track changes over time.

    • Eye-opening data. The Sleep app doesn’t just keep track of your time asleep. It also shows how much time you spent in REM, Core, or Deep sleep, and helps create a schedule and a bedtime routine so you can meet your sleep goals.

    • Sleep apnea notifications. Sleep apnea is a condition where breathing momentarily stops during sleep. If untreated, it can potentially lead to hypertension, type 2 diabetes, and cardiac issues. Ultra 2 can now monitor breathing disturbances and notify you of possible sleep apnea. Making it easier to get the help you need for better rest and recovery.11

    • Keeps an eye on your heart. The ECG app is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram.12 And with the Heart Rate app, you can get notifications for high and low heart rate as well as irregular rhythm.13

    • Cycle Tracking. Apple Watch tracks your wrist temperature while you sleep, so you can see changes over time.14 Cycle Tracking uses this data to provide a retrospective estimate of when you likely ovulated, which can be helpful for family planning.15

    Intelligent ways to stay connected.

    Smart Stack. Widgets show up automatically based on time, location, and more. Like giving you a heads up if rain is coming.

    Live Activities. Stay on top of your activities and interests, including when your Uber is arriving or the latest sports scores.

    Translate. Ultra 2 intelligently suggests a Translate widget when traveling somewhere with a different language. See pronunciations using the Latin alphabet. Download languages to your watch.16 It’s the world on your wrist.

    Precision Finding. A second-generation Ultra Wideband chip enables Precision Finding for iPhone 15 or later. It gives you the approximate distance and direction to your phone with helpful haptic feedback.17

    Apple Pay. Send money to friends with Apple Cash18 or pay for just about anything right from your wrist with Apple Pay.19 And use Wallet as your ID, home key, car key, or boarding pass.20

    The freedom of cellular.

    Call and text. Cellular is built into every Ultra 2.21 With a service plan, you can keep in touch almost anywhere, even if you’ve left your iPhone behind for a run. And stay connected abroad with an international roaming plan.22

    Music. Stream your favorite music, podcasts, and audiobooks from the top of a mountain or at the gym.23

    Check-in. Going for a run after dark? Check In keeps an eye on you for the duration of your workout and automatically notifies your friend when you finish.24

    Keep help close at hand.

    Emergency SOS. Press and hold the side button to quickly contact emergency services and provide them with your location.8 For a period of time, your emergency contacts will get updates when your location changes.

    Fall Detection. Whether you’re at home or out adventuring, if a fall is detected and you’re unresponsive, a call to emergency services will be placed automatically.8

    Crash Detection. Advanced sensors and a machine learning algorithm work to detect severe car crashes and contact emergency services.8 Ultra 2 also provides your location to dispatchers and notifies your emergency contacts.
