    Design and Display

    Thrill. Sleeker.

    The all-new iPad Pro packs astonishing power into an unbelievably thin, light, and portable design. Push the limits of what’s possible on iPad with a superportable 11-inch model and an expansive 13-inch model that is the thinnest product Apple has ever created.

    Thrill. Sleeker.

    A powerhouse of portability.

    iPad Pro combines next‑level performance with an ultrasleek design so you can do your best work anywhere you go. The 13‑inch model is nearly a quarter of a pound lighter than the previous generation, giving you unmatched capability and portability.

    Gorgeous elements all around.

    iPad Pro comes in two great finishes — Silver and new Space Black. The durable design is made of 100 percent recycled aluminum in the enclosure.

    iPad Pro includes 100 percent recycled rare earth elements in all magnets.

    The world’s most advanced display.

    The new Ultra Retina XDR display introduces groundbreaking tandem OLED technology. Extreme brightness, incredibly precise contrast, and advanced technologies like ProMotion and True Tone give you a jaw-dropping visual experience. And you can use Reference Mode for color-accurate workflows.

    The world’s most advanced display.

    OLED it shine.

    The Ultra Retina XDR display delivers extraordinary full-screen brightness and unparalleled precision built on breakthrough tandem OLED technology. The combined light of two OLED panels generates amazing brightness and extreme dynamic range performance.

    Peak visual performance.

    The color and luminance of each pixel is controlled down to the submillisecond, enabling next‑level XDR performance. The Ultra Retina XDR display is even more responsive to content in motion.

    Precision specular highlights convey more true-to-life details and accurate reflections of light in every image. So breathtaking HDR landscapes, textures, and tones feel even more vibrant and realistic.

    2,000,000:1 contrast ratio delivers deeper blacks for extreme dynamic range. There is pinpoint brightness in the stars and incredible detail in low light and shadows.

    Nano-texture glass.

    Brilliant in any light.

    Nano‑texture display glass is a new option that’s perfect for high‑end, color-managed workflows or the most demanding ambient lighting environments. Precisely etched at a nanometer scale, nano‑texture glass maintains image quality and contrast while scattering ambient light for even less glare.

    M4 chip

    Rocket chip.

    Introducing M4, the next generation of Apple silicon. M4 powers Apple Intelligence on iPad Pro, helping you write, express yourself, and get things done effortlessly. And it delivers outrageous performance in an exceptionally thin and light design. An entirely new display engine enables stunning precision, color, and brightness. A powerful GPU with hardware-accelerated ray tracing renders game-changing graphics. And the Neural Engine in M4 makes iPad Pro an absolute powerhouse for AI.

    Learn more about Apple Intelligence

    M4.
    Rocket chip.

    Extreme performance by design.

    M4 is built on second-generation 3-nanometer technology that’s even more power efficient and delivers all-day battery life.5 The CPU includes up to four performance cores and six efficiency cores for advanced tasks like instantly adding demanding effects to 4K video.6

    M4 compared to previous iPad Pro models with M2

    10-core CPU performance4
    120 GB/s memory bandwidth
    3D render performance3

    Graphics at light speed.

    Ray tracing on

    Ray tracing off

    The next-generation GPU architecture of M4 brings even faster rendering performance and hardware-accelerated ray tracing to iPad for the first time. So light, shadows, and reflections in pro apps and games look incredibly vibrant and realistic.

    Dynamic Caching optimizes on‑chip memory to increase power efficiency and boost graphics performance in all kinds of apps and games.

    Hardware-accelerated mesh shading uses improved geometry processing to enable even more visually complex scenes.

    AI by the power of M4.

    The M4 Neural Engine takes AI and machine learning in iPadOS to the next level and powers Apple Intelligence throughout the system. Capable of an astounding 38 trillion operations per second, M4 lets you do things like isolate a subject from its background in 4K video with just a tap in Final Cut Pro or automatically create musical notation in real time as you play a piano using StaffPad.7

    Watch and work in high resolution.

    A more advanced Media Engine with hardware acceleration for AV1 allows more power-efficient playback of high-resolution video.

    Play back and edit up to five streams of 4K ProRes footage. From sophisticated 3D rendering to graphics-heavy post-production, you can take on advanced workflows at the studio or on location.

    iPadOS and apps. Workflow workhorses.

    iPadOS is designed to let you power through advanced workflows and do all the things you love with ease and simplicity. Run pro apps, play high-performance games, and take on creative projects of any size with an intuitive touch-first experience.

    Shown above: Stage Manager, Final Cut Pro for iPad, Procreate Dreams, Octane X, Logic Pro for iPad, Procreate, Freeform, Morpholio Trace, Photos, Shapr3D

    iPadOS and apps
    Workflow workhorses.

    All done. With the power of touch.

    iPadOS is easy to use and designed for the unique capabilities of iPad. Intuitive touch navigation makes it all a breeze — even for the most pro workflows. And iPadOS takes advantage of the accelerated machine learning abilities of M4 to enable advanced AI features across a broad array of apps.

    Apps for all kinds of amazing.

    Pro apps unleash the extraordinary things you can do with iPad Pro. With essential built-in apps and over a million apps on the App Store designed specifically for the unique capabilities of iPad, iPadOS is an ideal platform for work, play, and creativity.

All with pixel‑perfect precision, low latency, tilt sensitivity, and support for palm rejection. And Apple Pencil Pro adds even more capabilities to bring your ideas to life like never before.1

    Apple Pencil Pro. Engineered for limitless creativity.

    Intuitive and precise creative control.

    Squeeze.

    Apple Pencil Pro can sense when you squeeze it and brings up a palette, so you can quickly switch tools, line weights, and colors.

    Barrel roll.

    A gyroscope allows you to rotate Apple Pencil Pro for precise control of shaped pen and brush tools.

    Haptic feedback.

    A custom haptic engine provides precise feedback that you can feel. When you squeeze or double‑tap, you’ll feel a light pulse confirming the action.

    Apple Pencil hover.

    Preview exactly where your Apple Pencil will touch down on your display and see a virtual shadow of your tool. So you can write, sketch, and illustrate with even greater precision.

    Double tap.

    Quickly switch between tools, like a pen and eraser, with a double tap on Apple Pencil Pro.

    Ready when inspiration strikes.

    Apple Pencil Pro pairs, charges, and stores magnetically on the side of iPad Pro. And if you lose or misplace it, you can easily locate your Apple Pencil Pro in the Find My app.

    iPad Pro works with Apple Pencil Pro and Apple Pencil (USB‑C).

    Two great options for all kinds of creativity.

    Magic Keyboard.
    Innovation is key.

    Magic Keyboard delivers an amazing typing and trackpad experience in a sleek, portable new design with an aluminum palm rest.1 The keyboard includes a function row and a larger glass trackpad with haptic feedback for precise work and Multi‑Touch gestures.

    Learn more about keyboards for iPad

    Magic
    Keyboard.
    Innovation is key.

    Sleek profile.
    Chic protection.

    Magic Keyboard comes in two colors that perfectly match iPad Pro. It features machined aluminum, a durable outer cover, and a USB‑C connector for pass-through charging.1 iPad attaches to Magic Keyboard magnetically and the Smart Connector immediately connects power and data without the need for Bluetooth, so it’s ready to use instantly.

    Full function row and then some.

    Type comfortably on an advanced design with backlit keys, smoothly adjust angles to your comfort, and use the new 14-key function row for convenient access to features like screen brightness or volume controls.

    Precision you can feel.

    Navigate large spreadsheets and comfortably highlight text with a bigger, redesigned glass trackpad with haptic feedback. Conveniently scroll, swipe, or use Multi‑Touch gestures for even more productivity.

    Cameras.
    Action.

    Shoot, edit, and share all on one device with a pro camera system with LiDAR Scanner, studio-quality mics, and four speakers. Capture 4K ProRes video, hop on a video call with the new landscape front camera, and take dramatically better document scans enhanced by AI.

    • 12MP Wide camera with adaptive True Tone flash.

    • Landscape 12MP Center Stage camera puts you front and center.

    • Studio-quality mics for great video calls.

    Mics. Cameras. Action.

    Shoot, edit, and share the beauty.

    The advanced 12MP Wide camera makes iPad Pro a complete mobile studio for creators. Capture detailed 4K ProRes video and vibrant Smart HDR images with even better color, improved textures, and increased detail in low light.

    Scanning gets smarter.

    The new adaptive True Tone flash dramatically improves document scanning on iPad Pro. The camera uses AI to detect documents like forms and receipts. If a shadow is in the way, it takes multiple images with the True Tone flash and stitches them together to create a high-quality scan without shadows or glare.

    Measure and model it with a tap.

    The LiDAR Scanner greatly enhances 3D workflows and enables faster and more immersive AR experiences. So you can turn a quick scan into a 3D CAD project that you can edit in Trimble SketchUp — all from one device.

    Stay front and center.

    The Landscape 12MP Center Stage camera is perfect for video calls with Center Stage. And it works with the TrueDepth camera to securely unlock iPad Pro with Face ID.

    Grab the mics.
    Turn up the volume.

    Record and play back rich audio with four studio-quality microphones and a four-speaker audio system with support for Dolby Atmos.

    Connectivity

    Dream big.
    Connect fast.

    Pro connectivity adds a whole new dimension of versatility to iPad Pro. Drive high-resolution external displays up to 6K and connect to storage devices with Thunderbolt. And stay connected wherever you are with blazing-fast Wi-Fi 6E and 5G capabilities.8

    Dream big. Connect fast.

    Plug it in. Go all out.

    Thunderbolt / USB 4 enables connectivity to high-performance storage devices, docks, and external displays.1

    Wireless connectivity. Blazing fast.

    • Wi-Fi 6E delivers incredibly fast wireless connectivity so you can stream, download, and share at high speeds.8

    • 5G cellular capabilities let you stay connected to the fastest wireless networks when you’re on the go.8

    • Now exclusively eSIM, enabling a fast, convenient, and secure way to activate your cellular data plan directly on iPad Pro.9

      Learn more about cellular for iPad

    • Bluetooth 5.3 low-latency audio supports next-generation headsets and hearing aids that use Bluetooth LE.

    What matters to you matters to Apple, too. From using more recycled materials that minimize environmental impact. To privacy protections that give you more control over your data. To creating built‑in features that make iPad accessible to all.

    The new iPad Pro has a durable design that uses 100 percent recycled aluminum in the enclosure.

    Privacy is a fundamental human right. Which is why we design our products and services to protect it.

    The best technology works for everyone. That’s why our products and services are inclusive by design.

    Designed to make a difference.

    Big steps toward a smaller footprint.

    iPad Pro contains more recycled materials than ever:

    • 100 percent recycled aluminum in the durable enclosure.
    • 100 percent recycled rare earth elements in all iPad Pro magnets.
    • 100 percent fiber-based packaging for iPad Pro, with 100 percent recycled or responsibly sourced wood fibers.10
    Our stores, offices, and data centers are already carbon neutral. We’ve committed to making our entire carbon footprint neutral by 2030.

    The Health app makes it easy for you to access your health and fitness information — and encrypts it on device and in iCloud so no one can view your Health data without your permission.

    Passkeys eliminate the need for passwords on apps and websites. Just use Touch ID or Face ID to authenticate. Simple and secure.

    Safari Private Browsing locks browsing windows when they’re not being used, blocks known trackers from loading, and removes identifying tracking data added to URLs as you browse.

    VoiceOver gives audible descriptions of what’s on your screen — from battery level to who’s calling to which app your finger is on. You can also adjust the speaking rate and pitch to suit your needs.
