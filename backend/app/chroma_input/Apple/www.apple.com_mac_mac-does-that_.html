    Mac does that

    Mac
    does  that.

    Never used a Mac? No problem. Mac is easy to learn and easy to use. And if youre considering the switch to Mac from PC, getting started is a breeze.

    Youll feel
    at home in no time.

    • If you love iPhone, youll love Mac. 

      Mac + iPhone

      If you love iPhone, youll love Mac. Mac is designed to be just as easy to learn as iPhone. And when you use them together, you can do incredible things.

      • See and use whats on your iPhone from your Mac without having to reach for it with iPhone Mirroring.1

      • Send and receive text messages from your Mac with iMessage.2

      • Copy text, images, videos, or files from your iPhone and paste them on your Mac with Universal Clipboard.

      • Take a picture or scan a document on your iPhone and see it instantly on your Mac with Continuity Camera.

      • Share your iPhone internet connection with your Mac using Personal Hotspot.

      Learn more about how Apple products work together

    • macOS.
      Quick to learn.
      Easy to love.

      macOS

      macOS. Quick to learn. Easy to love. Simple, intuitive gestures. Snappy, smooth multitasking. Free, seamless updates. Thats thanks to macOS the powerful, friendly operating system that makes a Mac a Mac. If youre switching over from PC, youll catch on quick.

      Explore the macOS User Guide

      Menu bar. Always at the top of your screen, so you can quickly find your controls.
      Dock. Easily choose and access your mostused apps. Simply drag and drop app icons to set up the Dock just the way you like it.
      Window buttons. Click red to close, yellow to minimize, or green to go full screen.

    • Your apps
      wont

      just run. Theyll fly.

      Apps

      Your apps wont just run. Theyll fly. When you switch to Mac, you can still use the apps you know and love like Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. And youll see just how fast, smooth, and snappy Slack, Zoom, and Google Docs can be. Mac also easily runs your favorite pro apps like Adobe Lightroom and Canva. Find these and thousands more in the Mac App Store.

      Check out the Mac App Store

    • Makes moving over files all smiles. 

      Setup

      Makes moving over files all smiles. Setting up a new Mac is easy. And if you have an iPhone, youre already part of the way there. Just bring your iPhone nearby, and Setup Assistant can automatically sign you into your Apple Account. It will transfer files, photos, messages, passwords, and more from iCloud right to your new Mac.

      If youre transferring from a PC, Migration Assistant will walk you through the process and put your content right in place on Mac. Once everything is transferred over, your new Mac will be ready with what you need. And you can run Migration Assistant at first boot up, or any time after.

    The marvels
    of Mac.

    • Multitask.
      Megafast.

      Performance

      Multitask. Megafast. Get more done faster from the moment you wake your Mac thanks to the power of Apple silicon. Navigate through hundreds of browser tabs. Design stunning presentations. Edit 4K videos. And do it all without slowing down.

    • Apple Intelligence.
      Personal, private, powerful.

      Apple Intelligence

      Apple Intelligence. Personal, private, powerful. Apple Intelligence is the personal intelligence system that helps you write, express yourself, and get things done effortlessly. With groundbreaking privacy protections, it gives you peace of mind that no one else can access your data not even Apple.5

      Learn more about Apple Intelligence

      • Siri. Siri draws on Apple Intelligence for all-new superpowers. With an all-new design, richer language understanding, and the ability to type to Siri whenever its convenient for you, communicating with Siri is more natural than ever. And with extensive product knowledge and the ability to tap into ChatGPT when it might be helpful to answer your question, Siri can assist you in brandnew ways.

      • Writing Tools. Use Apple Intelligence to proofread your text, rewrite different versions until the tone and wording are just right, and summarize selected text with a click. Or use Compose to tap directly into ChatGPT to create content from scratch.

      • Priority messages. Mail summarizes messages so you can get the gist of an email without even opening it. And get time-sensitive messages at the top of your inbox like an invitation for a meeting in an hour or a contract that needs your signature by midnight.

      • Image Playground. Create fun, original images based on a description, a concept, or even a person from your Photos library.

      Great powers come with great privacy. Apple Intelligence is designed to protect your privacy at every step. Its integrated into the core of your Mac through on-device processing. So its aware of your personal information without collecting your personal information.

      And with groundbreaking Private Cloud Compute, Apple Intelligence can draw on larger server-based models, running on Apple silicon, to handle more complex requests for you while protecting your privacy.

    • Up to 24 hours
      of battery life.6
      No charge.

      Battery

      Up to 24 hours of battery life. No charge. With all-day battery life, your Mac wont quit until you do and its performance wont change when its not plugged in. When its time to plug in, your Mac laptop battery can charge up to 50 percent in just 30 minutes.6

    • Mac was made
      for AI.

      AI

      Mac was made for AI. Thanks to Apple silicon, Mac brings you incredible AI performance. So you can harness all that AI has to offer and get more done every day.

      • Apple silicon makes it possible. The Neural Engine, along with a powerful CPU, GPU, and unified memory, makes for efficient handling of sophisticated AI processing. Mac uses the power of AI to boost your creativity and productivity. Which means your Mac excels at things like language processing, so you can use speech to text, and object recognition, so you can retouch photos or edit videos.

      • Privacy and security come first. Advanced security and privacy are built in to encrypt and protect your data. On Mac, you can run AI on device, so your inputs and results stay yours and theyre never stored.

      • A world of apps. An entire catalog of AI-ready apps is available on Mac, iPhone, and iPad, so you can boost your capabilities right away and work seamlessly across devices. For example, you can check homework with AI Math Assistance in Goodnotes, automatically enhance photos in Pixelmator Pro, and remove background noise from a video using Adobe Premiere Pro.

    • Your business is
      nobody elses.

      Privacy + Security

      Your business is nobody elses. Every Mac comes with industry-leading encryption, robust virus protection, and a powerful firewall system. Free and automatic security updates also help keep your Mac protected. And you control what data you share and how you share it, so you can use apps more securely, protect your information, and keep yourself safer on the web.

      • Touch ID. Use your finger to unlock your Mac, sign in to accounts, and authorize payments for purchases. Thanks to the Secure Enclave, your fingerprint data stays safe.

      • Find My. Locate your misplaced Mac and remotely lock or erase it if needed. The Find My app works even when your Mac is offline or powered off.

    • Brilliance.
      On display.

      Display

      Brilliance. On display. Rich, immersive, and vibrant, every Mac display ensures that your content looks its best. Photos and videos pop with rich contrast and sharp detail, and text is supercrisp for easy reading.

      • Glare, beware. A nano-texture display option is available on iMac and MacBook Pro. It reduces reflections and glare to eliminate distractions all while maintaining outstanding image quality. Its ideal for workspaces with challenging lighting conditions, like bright light sources or working outside.

      • Show your true colors. True Tone automatically adapts the warmth of your display to match the ambient light, to make colors appear consistent in different lighting conditions.

    For those who value value.

    • Designed to
      go the distance.

      Durability

      Designed to go the distance. Made of seriously strong aluminum, Mac computers are exceptionally well built and designed to last.

    • Free updates
      keep you up and running.

      Reliability

      Free updates keep you up and running. Free, regular software updates help avoid crashes and keep your Mac  and the apps you rely on  fast and responsive, ensuring that it runs smoothly and securely for years to come. These updates also deliver new features that can make an already incredible Mac even better.

      Heres how Mac stays up to date

    • Trade it forward.

      Trade-in

      Trade it forward. With Apple Trade In, you can get a great value for your old Apple device and apply it toward a new one. And you can do it online or at an Apple Store. If your device isnt eligible for credit, well recycle it for free. Well recycle your old PC for free as well.

      Heres how Apple Trade In works

      your Mac
      over time,
      interest-free.

      When you choose to check out with Apple Card Monthly Installments.

      Financing

      Pay for your Mac over time, interestfree. When you use your Apple Card and choose to check out with Apple Card Monthly Installments, you can pay for your new Mac with low monthly payments and with a 0% APR.

      Apple Card lives on your iPhone in the Wallet app. Apply in as little as a minute and see if youre approved. And with every purchase, you get up to 3% Daily Cash back.
